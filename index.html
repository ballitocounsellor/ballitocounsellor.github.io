Often people feel alone and overwhelmed when dealing with the multiple stressors that arise in everyday life and one cannot foresee the obstacles we are faced with.

Whether trying to adjust to a major life change, relationship or self-esteem issues or struggling to cope with the loss of a loved one, often all we need is someone who can help us feel less alone when dealing with these issues. The purpose of therapy is to provide you with an empathetic, non-judgmental environment in which you can explore the difficulties you are facing and also assist you in developing the tools that will ultimately allow you to better manage the situation.
My role as a Registered Counsellor is to facilitate such an environment and to support you while together, you figure out how best to deal with your particular situation.

Areas of influence include:

  • Pastoral Counselling
  • Grief Counselling
  • Couple’s Guidance and Counselling
  • Adolescents and Parenting
  • Trauma
  • Anxiety and Stress
  • Depression
  • Life adjustments, decision making or change

A little more about me

I am registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa and BHP. I manage a private practice in Ballito. In addition to my private work, I am affiliated to national Employee Wellness companies, providing group sessions and topic talks in a Corporate environment. I lecture part-time and also tutor for an online university. I also offer voluntary counselling to Grace Family Church. My work is varied and diverse, from private individual one-on-one sessions, to online sessions and counselling to larger groups.

I also offer guidance to numerous schools on topics that are relevant to the situations that our children are exposed to today. I am part of a local supervision forum, which assists me in remaining current in my outlook and accountable in my conduct. 

What is important to me

  • To ensure that clients find me approachable and feel able to discuss their issues openly and freely
  • To build rapport and trust with all medical professionals I interact with, and to appropriately refer clients where required
  • To treat all people with dignity and respect
  • To work in collaboration with my client to achieve their goals
  • To focusing ultimately on holistic health and well-being
