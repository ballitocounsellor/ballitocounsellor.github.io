A little more about me

I am registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa and manage a private practice in Ballito. In addition to my private work, I offer voluntary services to Umhlali SAPS Trauma Unit, as well as to Grace Family Church. My work is varied and diverse, from private individual sessions, to large group sessions in a Corporate environment.

I also offer guidance to numerous schools on topics that are relevant to the situations that our children are exposed to today. I lecture part-time and am part of the local Ballito Private Practitioners Forum which assists me in remaining current in my outlook and accountable in my conduct.
